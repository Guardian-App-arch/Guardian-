Overview

The project is an AI-powered tool called Guardian designed to detect dangerous situations and instantly alert relevant parties such as police, hospitals, or family members. By continuously analyzing ambient audio, Guardian identifies threats and ensures timely notification, improving response to dangerous situations. Guardian utilizes HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for a responsive front-end, with PHP handling back-end operations. Audio transcription is powered by the Whisper (base.en) AI model, while Ollama running Phi3.5 analyzes the transcribed text for signs of danger. In the event of a threat, Twilio is used to dispatch immediate SMS alerts.

The Flow

  • When activated, the recording starts. A 15seconds of the recording is sent for transcription
  • The 15 seconds of the recording is transcribed to text by Whisper (base.en) model.
  • The text is then analyzed by phi3.5 for sign of danger.
  • In case of danger, an alert (SMS messages) is sent to whom it may concern. If no danger is detected, the process is repeated until deactivated.
